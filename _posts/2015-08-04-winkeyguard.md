**Features:**  

  - Disables the Windows Key and rebinds it to a hotkey. (Useful when you are in full screen games.)  
  - Hold F2+F4 for 1.5 seconds to Simulate the Windows Key.  
  - Hold F2+F12 for 1.5 seconds to Exit.
